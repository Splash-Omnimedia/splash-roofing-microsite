Inside The Session

What We Work Through In One Focused Strategy Session

Six focus areas. One working day. Built around the decisions your roofing leadership team needs to make next.

01

Business Goals & Growth Stage

Clarify where the roofing company is trying to grow, what is changing, and what leadership needs marketing to support.

02

Audience, Service Mix & Markets

Identify the highest-value customers, service lines, locations, and opportunities — and decide what gets priority.

03

Website, Message & Conversion Path

04

Visibility & Lead Generation

Evaluate SEO, Google Business Profile, Google Ads, Local Service Ads, reviews, and referral sources.

05

Sales Follow-Up & Nurture

Look at what happens after a lead comes in — speed, messaging, CRM, email, and missed opportunities.

06

Priorities, Roadmap & Next Steps

Define the smartest next moves and what should happen first — so leadership leaves aligned, not overwhelmed.

Is This The Right Fit?
