Our Approach

No Pressure. No Random Sales Pitch.

This first step is about understanding where your roofing company is, what you are trying to improve, and whether Splash is the right fit to help. If the best next step is not obvious yet, that is exactly what the strategy conversation is designed to clarify.

  • We do not promise guaranteed rankings, leads, or revenue.
  • We do not recommend tactics before understanding the bigger picture.
  • We want to understand the business goal behind the marketing need.
  • If Splash is not the right fit, the team should be honest about that.
